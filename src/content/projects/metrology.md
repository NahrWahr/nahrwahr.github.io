# 3D Metrology & Localisation

Successfully developed an **IR camera-based 3D localisation system** designed for high-precision satellite testbeds.

## Engineering Highlights
- **Precision:** Achieved a localization accuracy of **$\leq 1\,mm$**.
- **Algorithm:** Implemented **SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping)** for real-time tracking.
- **Cost Effectiveness:** While commercial SOTA systems like Vicon cost upwards of **$\approx$ \$100k**, this custom solution was engineered for approximately **\$1k**, representing a significant breakthrough in accessibility for research labs.
