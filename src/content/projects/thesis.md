# MS Thesis: Phase Interpolation Research

Currently exploring novel architectures for **35GHz to 70GHz Phase Interpolation** that bypass the conventional requirement for I/Q (In-phase / Quadrature) clock generation.

## Research Objectives
- **Clock Generation Efficiency:** Reducing the power and area overhead associated with high-frequency I/Q generation.
- **Spectrum Coverage:** Targetting the mmWave bands for next-generation communication standards.
- **Architectural Innovation:** Implementing phase interpolation natively on the primary clock path.
