# Magneto-Hydro-Dynamic (MHD) Stirrer

Engineered an **Electromagnetic (E-M) pump** and stirrer system for industrial molten Aluminum furnaces.

## Project Scope
- **Prototype Development:** Modelled and optimized the architecture for a **$10\,kW$ test unit** within a three-month development cycle.
- **Multiphysics Modeling:** Conducted extensive simulations to ensure the design met strict thermal, power, and electromagnetic performance constraints.
- **Industrial Application:** Developed a foundational understanding of field analysis for heavy industrial drive systems at NeoCambrian.
